About the HashCon

Step into the future of cybersecurity with Hashcon, a groundbreaking conference collaboratively initiated by the Cyber Security Club and OWASP IIIT Kottayam. This innovative event serves as a nexus for students, research scholars, and industry leaders, fostering an exchange of cutting-edge insights in this rapidly evolving field.
Hashcon features expert-led sessions, hands-on training, internship opportunities, a Resume Clinic, a cyber exhibition, a Hackathon, and CTFs, along with diverse stalls covering attacks, companies, research, and projects. Beyond the technical aspects, we provide engaging and interactive student talks, a Cyber Night, cultural events, and more.Join us for an immersive experience where knowledge meets excitement, and the future of cybersecurity unfolds.
